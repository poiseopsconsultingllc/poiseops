Story

Why PoiseOps exists

PoiseOps started with a simple question: what if your workday did not have to feel like constant catch up.

Before launching PoiseOps, I spent more than a decade in grants, finance and accounting inside universities and schools of medicine. I lived in the world of sponsored projects, shifting requirements, tight deadlines and messy handoffs between departments.

Over those years, I became the systems person by necessity. I built and fixed workflows in Excel, Power Query, SQL, Power BI and Tableau, then moved into Python, AI and automation to stop doing the same manual tasks over and over.

PoiseOps is the result of that journey — a way to use what I learned to make other people’s workdays calmer, clearer and more sustainable.

I come from a military family and I have a deep respect for people who serve and for the people who keep public, grant funded and community work going behind the scenes.

Why work with PoiseOps.

  • I have lived on your side of the screen. I understand grants, budgets, sponsors, finance and reporting from the inside.
  • I speak both money and machines. I connect real-world constraints with tools like Python, SQL, Excel, Power Automate, Power BI and Tableau.
  • I build on what you already use. Outlook/Gmail, Google Workspace and your existing data are the starting point.
  • The goal is poise, not more hustle. More control and breathing room, less noise.
  • You get founder attention. The same person who listens designs, builds, and tunes the system.
  • Pricing is right-sized. Projects that pay for themselves in time saved and reduced overhead.
