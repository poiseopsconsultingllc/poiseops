Process

How PoiseOps works

This is not a giant consulting project. It is a focused, step by step reset of how your work flows.

1
Discovery
We get honest about what is actually happening in your day.

We start with a short call or screen share where you walk me through real life, not the polished version:

  • How your email looks when you open it on a busy morning
  • Where proposals, grants or approvals keep getting stuck
  • Which spreadsheets, databases or dashboards you have to open just to answer basic questions

You talk and share your screen. I ask questions. Together we find the few spots that are doing the most damage to your time, budget and energy.

2
Design
We sketch what a calmer version of your workday looks like.

Next, I turn what we saw into a clear, simple plan. Nothing heavy, no fifty page document, just a practical map that answers:

  • What can stay manual and what should be automated
  • Which tools we will lean on, like Outlook, Gmail, Google Workspace, Excel, SQL, Power Query, Power BI, Power Automate and Python
  • What the new flow will be from incoming work through decisions to reporting
  • What success looks like in plain language, including time saved and overhead avoided

You see the plan, you ask questions and we adjust until it feels realistic and safe, not like some theoretical system you will hate in a week.

3
Build
I do the heavy lifting so you can keep doing your actual job.

Once the plan is clear, I go build. Depending on what you need, that might include:

  • Reworking inbox rules, folders and labels so important messages stand out
  • Creating templates, automations and AI assisted workflows around email, proposals and grants
  • Cleaning data, writing SQL and wiring up dashboards in Power BI or Tableau
  • Setting up small Python scripts or low code flows so information moves between your tools without copy paste

You do not have to manage the technical details. You just test with me as we go and tell me if it feels right.

4
Poise and tuning
We launch, watch it in real life and adjust until it truly supports you.

When everything is in place, we turn it on in your real world. For a period after launch, I stay close so we can:

  • Fix any rough edges that only show up once people start using the new flow
  • Adjust rules, timing and reports so they match how your team actually behaves
  • Add light documentation or quick videos so you and your team feel confident

The goal is not just to ship a system. The goal is that a month from now you feel calmer, more in control and less buried, even if funding slows or the news gets weird.
